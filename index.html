Gray wolf

The saliva of gray wolves is thought to reduce bacterial infections in wounds.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus
  • Height: 80 – 85 cm
  • Lifespan: 5 – 6 years
  • Conservation status : Least Concern

Gray wolves range in color from grizzled gray or black to all-white. As the ancestor of the domestic dog, the gray wolf resembles German shepherds. Though they once nearly disappeared from the lower 48 states, today wolves have returned to the Great Lakes, northern Rockies and South western United States.
